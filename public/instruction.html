About Sokudo

Hello, we are Sakura Team, and we are happy to present You our new app. Maybe, You have already noticed, that our app's name is Sokudo or speed in Japanese.
Sokudo allows You to download materials from a lot of platforms for free! Yeah, it's all free. So if you don't know how to use our app there is an instruction below!

Instruction

1) Before downloading you need to choose the place, where you want to download your material. It is button with folder-icon.
2) Also you can change some preferences, just press button with cog-icon. After choosing your parameters press accept button. And on the main window press button with person-icon. If you want to use standard preferences just press button with star-icon. After every change of your preferences you need to press button with person-icon.
3) And finally tap on central button to download your information.
4) To stop downloading You just need to delete downloading file, exit the app, reload the page or open TikTok Downloader window.
